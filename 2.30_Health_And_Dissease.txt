Dissease => state of body when it is unable to maintain Homeostasis
Homeostasis => keeping optimum conditions for body functions

Signs => fever, rash
Symptoms => feeling unwell, fatigue

Non-infectious disseases:
    - Degenerative (age related) => effect of Free radicals
    - Deficiency
    - Allergy
    - Inherited

Infectious disseases:
    - Caused by pathogens
    - Transmisible
    - How to fight:
        - Antibiotics
        - Immunisation programmes
        - improved public Hygiene
