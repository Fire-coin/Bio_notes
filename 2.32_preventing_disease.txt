Food posisoning:
    - Microbes feed on host tissue when eaten
    - Microbes release toxin
    - Causes:
        - preparation:
            - contaminated water
            - poor Hygiene
        - storing:
            - uncooked / cooked foods stored together
    - foods most at risk:
        - cooked meat or rice
        - Shellfish
    - Symptoms:
        - Abdominal pain
        - Diarrhoea, Vomiting => dehydration
    - Prevention:
        - avoiding contamination
        - storing in fridges
        - cook well
